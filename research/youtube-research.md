# YouTube Research — Top 20 XC Ski Races

## Key Creators
- **Andreas Fausko** — Vasaloppet 45, Marcialonga 2025, Nordenskiöldsloppet 2025 (quality race vlogs, 15-21 min)
- **Magnus Waaler** — Vasaloppet 2024, Birken 2025 (POV/GoPro)
- **Oliver From Sweden** — Vasaloppet 2024, NattVasan 2025
- **Worldloppet** (channel) — NattVasan, Gatineau Loppet, Jizerská 50
- **SC - The Ski Community** — Official Ski Classics highlights for nearly all races
- **Rando Sui** — Birkebeinerrennet 2023, Tartu Maraton 2024 (long-form full race)

## Coverage Quality
- **Best**: Vasaloppet, Birkebeinerrennet, Nordenskiöldsloppet, Marcialonga
- **Limited**: Tervahiihto, Kangaroo Hoppet, Engadin La Diagonela, Skarverennet
