## Key Statistics

- **World's oldest organized ski race**: Tervahiihto (Finland, 1889)
- **World's longest single-stage race**: Nordenskioldsloppet (Sweden, 220km)
- **Largest by participation**: Vasaloppet (Sweden, ~16,000), Engadin Skimarathon (Switzerland, ~13,500), Skarverennet (Norway, 12,000+), American Birkebeiner (USA, ~10,000)
- **Smallest Worldloppet race**: Merino Muster (New Zealand, ~100)
- **Oldest Worldloppet race**: Vasaloppet (1922)
- **Newest Worldloppet race**: Demino (2007, now suspended)
- **Most extreme**: Arctic Circle Race (Greenland, -25C tent sleeping), Tana-Varangerrennet (Norway's northeasternmost)
- **Suspended**: Demino Ski Marathon (Russia), Ushuaia Loppet (Argentina)
- **Discontinued**: Rajalta Rajalle Hiihto (Finland, 420km, ended 2025)
